Biography

Komeil Moghaddasi (Member, IEEE) was born in West Azerbaijan, Iran, in 1993. He received the B.Sc. degree in computer engineering, in 2019, and the M.Sc. degree in information technology from Islamic Azad University, Urmia, Iran, in 2023. Since 2021 he has been a Research Assistant, where he works on distributed computing, edge intelligence, and electric‑vehicle energy management projects in collaboration with industry partners. He has authored more than 10 peer‑reviewed articles disseminated through IEEE, Elsevier and Springer venues. His work has attracted over 216 citations with an h‑index of 9, reflecting growing impact in his working field. He is a member of the IEEE Computer and Communications Societies and of ACM. He serves as a reviewer for leading peer‑reviewed journals published by IEEE, Elsevier, and Springer.
